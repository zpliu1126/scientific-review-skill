## Synthesis

The available sources converge on drought-responsive transcriptional remodeling in crop plants. Paper A provides RNA-seq and selected qRT-PCR/qPCR expression validation, while Paper B adds co-expression evidence linked to a drought-related trait. Neither source, based on the provided material, establishes causal gene function.
