## Synthesis

The evidence spans cellular perturbation, animal model phenotype, and human clinical association. Together, the sources support a plausible disease-related role, but they do not establish a complete human causal mechanism without rescue, mechanistic biochemical evidence, and replicated human cohort or therapeutic validation.
