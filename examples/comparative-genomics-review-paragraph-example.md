# Example: Comparative Genomics Review Paragraph

## Draft Paragraph

Comparative genomics can help identify conserved and lineage-specific candidates for stress-related gene family evolution. In the provided notes for Paper E, synteny analysis identified orthologous gene pairs across two crop species, while Paper F reported lineage-specific expansion of a transcription factor subfamily. These findings support the model synthesis that conserved syntenic genes and expanded lineage-specific members should be discussed as distinct evidence classes. However, orthology or gene expansion alone does not demonstrate conserved or novel function; functional claims require expression, perturbation, biochemical, or phenotype evidence. [source needed]
