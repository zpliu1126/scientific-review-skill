## Gene Classification Rules

- Functionally validated gene: supported by genetic perturbation, complementation, transgenic validation, biochemical/mechanistic evidence, field validation, or another explicit functional assay.
- Candidate gene: supported by mapping, association, sequence, expression, network, or database evidence but not experimentally validated for the focal function.
- Stress/trait-responsive gene: expression or abundance changes under a treatment, tissue, developmental stage, or phenotype contrast.
- Homolog-inferred gene: inferred from an ortholog or homolog in another species; not target-species validation.
- Database-annotated gene: included because of database annotation; not experimental evidence unless the database record provides a traceable source.
