# Plant Small RNAs

Plant small RNAs are sequence-specific regulatory RNAs commonly discussed in the 20-24 nt range in [[Axtell 2013 - Classification and Comparison of Small RNAs from Plants]]. They are loaded into AGO proteins and can guide repression through RNA destabilization, translational repression, or chromatin modification.
