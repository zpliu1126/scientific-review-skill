# Small Interfering RNA

In [[Axtell 2013 - Classification and Comparison of Small RNAs from Plants]], siRNA refers to small RNA derived from DCL-catalyzed processing of double-stranded RNA.

## Subclasses

- heterochromatic siRNA
- [[Phased secondary siRNA]]
- NAT-siRNA

## Evidence Boundary

> [!note]
> The review presents siRNA subclasses as useful for plant small RNA organization, but it also flags overlaps and unresolved mechanisms, especially between heterochromatic and secondary siRNA pathways.
