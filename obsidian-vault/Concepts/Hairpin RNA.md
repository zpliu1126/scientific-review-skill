# Hairpin RNA

In [[Axtell 2013 - Classification and Comparison of Small RNAs from Plants]], hpRNA refers to plant small RNAs derived from DCL-catalyzed processing of a self-complementary single-stranded precursor with a hairpin structure.

## Subclasses

- microRNA
- non-miRNA hpRNA

> [!warning]
> The review treats non-miRNA hpRNAs as less cohesive than canonical miRNAs. Mark specific examples as provisional unless direct biogenesis and function evidence are available.
